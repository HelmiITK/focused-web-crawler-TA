Direct Marketing Policy

Jurnal Informatika will provide accurate information about the journal and offerings and ensure that direct marketing efforts are targeted and relevant to recipients. Considering their previous interactions with Jurnal Informatika, the editors fit the recipients' interests and preferences. Jurnal Informatika will comply with all applicable laws and regulations related to direct marketing, including anti-spam and consumer protection laws.
